Prompt Examples & Best Practices

Email Writing - Professional

Task: Write a professional email to a client about a project delay.
Context: The delay was due to unexpected technical issues that are now resolved. The new delivery date is next Friday.
References: The tone should be professional but empathetic. Format as a subject line and a brief email body under 150 words.

Content Creation - Social Media

Task: Create 3 engaging Instagram post captions.
Context: For a new 30-day fitness challenge targeting busy professionals (25-40).
References: Use a motivational and encouraging tone, similar to major fitness brands. Include relevant hashtags.

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

‚ùå Vague Instructions

Don't: "Write something creative."

Do: "Write a 200-word story about a time-traveling chef in Victorian London."

‚ùå Word Vomiting

Don't: Put everything in one huge paragraph.

Do: Organize your prompt with clear sections like Task, Context, etc.

‚ùå One-Shot Mentality

Don't: Expect perfection on the first try.

Do: Plan to iterate and refine the prompt based on the AI's output.

Advanced Techniques

üîó Prompt Chaining

Connect multiple prompts to build complex solutions. The output of one prompt becomes the input for the next.

Chain Example - Content Strategy

1. Idea Generation: "List 10 trending topics in sustainable fashion for 2025."
2. Outlining: "Using the list above, create detailed outlines for the top 3 topics."
3. Drafting: "For the first outline, write a complete 800-word blog post."

ü§î Chain of Thought (CoT) Prompting

Ask the AI to "think step by step" to improve its reasoning on complex problems.

CoT Example - Problem Solving

"A SaaS company has 1000 free users but only a 5% conversion rate. The trial is 14 days. How would you improve this? Think through this systematically and explain your reasoning step-by-step."

ü§ñ AI Agent Creation

Create specialized AI assistants for specific tasks using a detailed persona and interaction rules.

Agent Example - Interview Prep

Persona: You are an experienced HR director at a top tech company.
Context: I'm preparing for a senior software engineer interview.
Interactions: Conduct a realistic interview. Ask challenging questions and provide feedback after each answer.
